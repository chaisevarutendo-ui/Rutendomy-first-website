CAREERS IN BIOMEDICAL ENGINEERING

Possible careers include:

Biomedical Engineer

Designs medical devices and equipment such as prosthetics and imaging machines .

Clinical engineer

Works in hospitals to manage and maintain medical technologies safely.

Research Scientist

Explores innovations in regenerative medicine, nanotechnology,and biotechnology.

Rehabilitation Engineer

Creates assistive devices and technologies for people with disabilities.

Biomaterials Specialist

Develops materials used in implants,tissue engineering and drug delivery systems.

Essential Skills and Qualifications

-Strong foundation in biology ,physics and engineering
-Knowledge of medical imaging and device design
-Software and Programming
-Knowledge of prototyping and testing
-Problem-solving and critical thinking
Teamwork and communicaton
